416A5 AERs are to be rounded and displayed up to two decimal places.

418A6 Where the AER varies according to the date of the deposit (for example, where
419 an unconditional bonus is offered until a fixed calendar date or a different
420 gross rate applies for deposits received on application pending investment
421 into the product):
422 • All advertising should include a statement that the AER assumes that investment was
423 made on a specified date.
424 • The date specified should be:
425 o relevant to the date the advertisement or literature will appear or be available
426 and not more than 1 month away from that date.
427 o for deposits where a different gross rate applies for money received on
428 application pending investment into the product – the specified date should be
429 that which the provider first expects to receive funds from customers.
430 • Advertisements showing a recalculated AER will need to be amended on a monthly
431 basis, unless the recalculated AER would be higher than the originally advertised
432 rate. (This allows providers some operational flexibility regarding the updating of
433 advertisements where a short-term period of underquoting AER can be justified given
434 the operational benefits).

436A7 Where regular monthly savings products are advertised which have a limited
437 life of one year and interest is only credited once a year the AER should not
438 take account of any interest earned after the account matures. The AER is
439 deemed to be the same as the nominal rate for the account.
